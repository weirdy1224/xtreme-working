About Cyber Synapse 2.0

Welcome to Cyber Synapse 2.0, where technology meets heroism! Just like LEGO superheroes unite to save the world, our event brings together brilliant minds to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.


Our prestigious institution has been nurturing technological heroes for over two decades, creating innovators who shape the digital universe. Cyber Synapse 2.0 represents our mission to build a community where students can assemble their skills, much like building the perfect LEGO creation.


Join us for an epic adventure featuring cutting-edge competitions, inspiring workshops, and networking opportunities that will transform you into the tech hero you're meant to be!

Hero Manual (FAQ)

What is Cyber Synapse 2.0?
Cyber Synapse 2.0 is an epic tech event inspired by LEGO superheroes! Featuring 6 heroic challenges - 3 technical (AI Challenge, Code Sprint, Web Wizardry) and 3 creative (Design Thinking, Tech Talk, Innovation Pitch) - designed to unleash your inner tech superhero.
How do I register for the hero challenges?
Click on any event card to view details, then hit the "Register Now" button in the popup. You can join multiple challenges to maximize your hero training! Registration closes on October 15th, 2025.

Can I participate in multiple hero challenges?
Absolutely! True heroes master multiple skills. You can register for as many challenges as you want, but make sure you can handle the hero training schedule as some events may overlap.
What hero training do I need?
Most challenges welcome heroes of all levels! Technical challenges require basic programming powers, while creative challenges need innovative thinking abilities. Complete training materials will be provided in your hero handbook upon registration.
What rewards await the heroes?
Heroes earn cash prizes, official certificates, and exclusive LEGO-themed merchandise! All participants receive hero completion certificates. Special awards recognize the most innovative solutions and inspiring presentations.
